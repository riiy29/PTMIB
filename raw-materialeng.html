What is Raw Materials ?

Raw Materials Raw materials are materials or substances used in the main production or manufacture of goods. Raw materials are commodities that are bought and sold on commodity exchanges around the world.

Products Sample

Palm sugar

Palm sugar is a natural sweetener product made from sap which is harvested from coconut trees and palm trees.

Refined Crystal Sugar

Refined sugar or white crystal sugar is raw sugar that has undergone a purification process to remove molasses so that refined sugar is whiter than raw sugar which is more brown in color.

Coconut

Coconut is the sole member of the Cocos clan. The meaning of the word coconut can refer to the whole coconut tree, seeds, or fruit .

Tepung Tapioka

Tapioca or sometimes called kanji is starch extracted from cassava tubers. Tapioca flour also has several other names, such as aci flour or starch .
