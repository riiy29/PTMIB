More About Us

Manufactures Goods

We sell manufactured goods and cooperate with the company. Example: Frisian Flag Sweetened Condensed Milk, ABC Soy Sauce, ABC Sauce, ABC Syrup etc.

Raw Materials

We provide various types of raw materials such as: coconut sugar, palm sugar, refined sugar, old coconut, tapioca flour, eggs, coffee, coconut briquettes, salt etc.

Health Related Goods

We sell all kinds of medical equipment that you will use every day, such as medical masks, gloves, and hand sanitizers. We Also Sell A Variety Of Medicines And Vitamins.

Distribution Details

We Distribute Goods With 4 business models.

B2B F&B providers using e-channel distribution

PT. Makmur Inti Barokah (PT. MIB) become registered vendor for Bizzy (Toko smart) – Sinarmas and provide well known products as per Bizzy requirement (e.g., Frisian Flag Indonesia, ABC Group, PT. Cerestar – flour products, PT. Susanti Megah – salt products, etc). Products delivered to Bizzy 33 Distribution Centre.

Wahyoo – start-up, PT. MIB become registered vendor and provide products, such as: sugar, rice, egg, etc. Products delivered to Wahyoo Hub.

B2B F&B direct to Corporation

PT. MIB become register for PT. PIDC – PELNI and provide biscuit and frozen meat products for both PELNI crews and passengers. Products delivered directly to each PELNI ship.

PT. MIB become register vendor for PT. Agrinesia Raya – producer of Lapis Talas Sangkuriang Bogor for raw materials product (egg, sugar, flour). Products delivered directly to PT. Agrinesia Raya factory.

Penyedia B2B F&B menggunakan distribusi ritel

PT. MIB menjadi vendor terdaftar untuk Aeon Supermarket menyediakan produk seperti fresh juice (Toza) dan aneka gula kelapa. Produk dikirim ke toko tertentu.

B2C provider through marketplace

PT. MIB has an official online store in the Tokopedia and Shopee marketplaces and Tokopedia. We provide a variety of "consumer goods" products (food&beverage, health&vitamins, etc.)
